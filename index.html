Why Trucert Certificates?

Immutable

Certificates are stored on a blockchain, making them tamper-proof and permanent.

Verifiable

Anyone can verify authenticity instantly by checking the blockchain record or scanning a QR code.

Secure

Using cryptographic hashes and decentralized storage prevents forgery or unauthorized revocation.

Benefits for Businesses

  • Cost-effective, eco-friendly alternative to paper certificates.
  • Builds trust with customers & partners.
  • Lifetime access to records; no loss or damage of documents.
  • Automated credential verification no manual work needed.

Technology Stack

Smart Contracts

Solidity contracts on Ethereum / Polygon manage certificate issuance & revocation.

Decentralized Storage

IPFS stores certificate images & metadata, linked via cryptographic hashes.

Frontend

HTML, CSS, JavaScript with Web3.js for seamless MetaMask integration and QR scanning.

Security

Only authorized addresses can issue certificates; public can verify but not modify.
